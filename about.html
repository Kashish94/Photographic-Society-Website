About Us

Who Are We ???

Officialy stands for "Photographic Society", it is one of the most zestful clubs of BIT Mesra. We are a team of 100 enthusiasts with a common love for photography and a desire to inculcate the same in 100 more such enthusiasts.

We work all year round; covering every event from a guest lecture to our grand fests: Pantheon, Vajra and Bitotsav.

What We Do ?



Graphic Design:

We have a bunch of people on our team proficient in Photoshop and Lightroom, conducting workshops to teach the same.


Videography:
The after-movie of all our fests is our domain.


Cinematography:
Participated and have won titles in the short film competition conducted in Bitotsav.


Video-editing:
We have a bunch of people on our team proficient in Ae and premiere pro, conducting workshops to teach the same.
